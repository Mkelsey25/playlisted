What even is this app? How do I use it? Why would I?
Playlisted:

Verb
To find the soundtrack of your life.

Playlisted is a music application that allows its users to discover and create the perfect playlist to fit their mood/activity level! Your first step towards finding the playlist of your dreams is to sign up for an account. After you've joined the Playlisted community, you will be presented with a simple personality quiz that will help design a playlist with you in mind. Don't like what you hear? Change it and rearrange it! Our app gives users the ability to customize their playlists with ease. Once you have found the perfect fit, save your playlist and listen on!
