Playlisted:

Audio Bliss
To find the soundtrack of your life.

Playlisted is a music application to discover and create the perfect playlist to fit a mood/activity level! Your first step towards finding the playlist of your dreams is to sign up for an account. After you join the Playlisted community, create your perfect playlists by selecting a mood, energy level, and genre. The personalized song recommendations are purpose built with you in mind. Don't like what you hear? Change it and rearrange it! Customize any playlists with ease. Once you have the perfect fit, save your playlist and listen on!
